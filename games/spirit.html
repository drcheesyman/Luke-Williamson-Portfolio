Overview

Split Spirit is a 2D puzzle platformer built in a custom engine. Play as a ball of light that can attatch to creatures to gain control over them. Solve puzzles and overcome environmental challenges to reach the top of the mountain. I worked as both a level and UX designer on this project, creating stages that utilize the environment to immerse the player and create challenges.
